About Halepo

The Inspiration

The name Halepo is inspired by the Aleppo pine (Pinus halepensis), an elegant and resilient tree species that has adorned the Mediterranean landscape of this region for centuries. This pine symbolizes natural beauty, durability, and the timeless charm of the Mediterranean lifestyle.

The Experience

In this unique project, we combine contemporary architecture, high-quality materials, and panoramic sea views, with a subtle nod to the rich nature and traditions of the area. Halepo stands not only for luxury and comfort but also for a connection to the environment.
