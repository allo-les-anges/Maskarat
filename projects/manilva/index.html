Discover Viña Manilva

Viña Manilva is an exclusive collection of elegant homes nestled between the Mediterranean Sea and the sun-drenched hills of Manilva, one of the Costa del Sol's best-kept secrets.

The name Viña Manilva pays tribute to the region's famous Moscatel vineyards, which have shaped the land and culture for generations.

This boutique development captures that same essence, combining tradition, nature, and contemporary Mediterranean living.

Welcome to Viña Manilva – where timeless charm meets modern elegance.

Prime Location

The villa offers a perfect blend of unspoiled natural tranquility, local amenities within easy reach, and an authentic Andalusian coastal experience ideal for those seeking relaxation with convenient access to shops, restaurants, and leisure activities.
