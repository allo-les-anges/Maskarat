Discover Viña Manilva

Viña Manilva is an exclusive collection of elegant homes nestled between the Mediterranean Sea and the sun-drenched hills of Manilva, one of the Costa del Sol's best-kept secrets.

The name Viña Manilva pays tribute to the region's famous Moscatel vineyards, which have shaped the land and culture for generations.

This boutique development captures that same essence, combining tradition, nature, and contemporary Mediterranean living.

Welcome to Viña Manilva – where timeless charm meets modern elegance.

The Viña Manilva Project

In Manilva's exclusive Guarda II Mare urbanisation, 12 high-quality townhouses are currently being developed. These homes combine contemporary comfort with energy efficiency and elegant finishes. Each residence includes a spacious basement with a private garage and a dedicated home cinema, an open-plan living area with direct access to the garden, and three bedrooms—each equipped with its own en-suite bathroom. Outside, residents can enjoy a generous private garden and a saltwater swimming pool, ideal for relaxation and leisure. Designed to blur the lines between indoor and outdoor living, these sustainable homes feature A-rated energy certification, a 1kWp solar energy system (expandable), and a climate-adapted architectural design to ensure optimal comfort and efficiency throughout the year.
